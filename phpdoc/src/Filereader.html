Extracts the arrival and leaving times of an visitor and puts them into an array



Gets a line from the source file as a parameter. Extracts from the line  (which is in format hh:mm,hh:mm where hh means hours and mm minutes in 24  hour format) the strings representing the arrival and leaving times of a  visitor. Uses Minute_String_Converter to convert those strings into an  integer value of minutes.
The arrival time is saved with a key "arrival" and the leaving time with  a key "leaving".
